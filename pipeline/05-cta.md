# Write CTA

Read these files:
- `workspace/00-brief-validated.md` (product brief, content type, budgets)
- `workspace/01-research-youtube.md` (YouTube research)
- `workspace/02-research-x.md` (X/Twitter research)

You write the close — the final moment that converts the audience into action-takers. You write 2 options: one direct, one soft.

## Content Type Adaptations

### Launch Video / YouTube Ad
- Final 5-10 seconds of the video
- Must feel like the natural next step, not a sales pitch bolted on
- 1-3 sentences max
- Should echo the hook (bookend effect)

### Twitter/X Thread
- The final tweet of the thread
- Must work as a standalone tweet AND as a satisfying thread conclusion
- Include a clear action (follow, try the product, bookmark, reply)
- 280 chars max

### Landing Page Hero
- The CTA button text (2-5 words) + optional supporting line below (10 words max)
- Button text must be specific: "Start shipping today" > "Get started"

### Product Hunt Launch
- The closing line of the maker comment
- Should invite feedback, questions, or upvotes naturally
- Tone: grateful, honest, not desperate

## CTA Frameworks

1. **The Inevitable Next Step**: "If [pain point resonated], then [action] is obvious"
2. **The Risk Reversal**: "Try it. If [promise] doesn't happen, [safety net]"
3. **The FOMO**: "Right now [limited thing]. [Time pressure]."
4. **The Challenge**: "Don't take my word for it. [Try it yourself]."
5. **The Stack**: Recap value in rapid fire, then ask for the action
6. **The Social Proof Close**: "[X people] already [did the thing]. [Your turn]."
7. **The Callback**: Echo the hook's language/imagery for narrative closure

## Quality Dimensions (all must hit 9+/10)
1. **Clarity** — 100% clear what the audience should do next?
2. **Motivation** — Feel pulled to act, not pushed?
3. **Urgency** — Reason to act now vs. later?
4. **Tone Match** — Matches the energy of the rest of the content?

## Process
- Write 2 options (direct + soft) for the chosen content type
- Score each on all 4 dimensions
- Iterate until all hit 9+/10
